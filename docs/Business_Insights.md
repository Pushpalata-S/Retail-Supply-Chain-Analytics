# Business Insights

## Executive Summary

The project analyzes retail sales performance using a normalized MySQL database and advanced SQL queries.

---

## Revenue & Profit

- Calculated total revenue and total profit across all orders.
- Computed Average Order Value (AOV).

---

## Customer Analysis

- Identified top customers based on revenue and profit.
- Measured customer order frequency.

---

## Product Analysis

- Ranked best-selling products.
- Identified the most profitable products.
- Compared category and sub-category performance.

---

## Regional Analysis

- Compared sales and profit across regions.
- Evaluated city and state level performance.

---

## Time Analysis

- Analyzed monthly sales trends.
- Calculated running revenue and moving averages using window functions.

---

## SQL Techniques Used

- Joins
- Aggregate Functions
- GROUP BY
- HAVING
- CASE
- CTEs
- Window Functions
- Views
- Indexes
- Query Optimization
